About Cruncher.Money

Cruncher.Money is a growing collection of simple, fast, mobile-friendly calculators to help people make smarter financial decisions.

The site is designed for clarity, speed, and usefulness — with no logins or intrusive ads. We aim to cover everyday needs from mortgage overpayments and home equity to savings, pensions, and financial independence.

Our tools are free to use. Some calculators may include links to trusted financial partners — if you click on one, we may earn a small referral fee which helps fund the site.

Cruncher.Money is an independent project and always aims to provide unbiased, useful tools for real people.
